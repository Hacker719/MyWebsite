County and State Science Fair

The CCSEF or California Science and Engineering Fair or 2019 was a tranformative fair for me. In the past I have won many awards for science fair projects, but they were all based on the scientific method. This year, I tried to combine CS and Science Fair to try something new. So I took a problem, which was that people dont recycle, and tried to fix that! I created a machine learning app (that can be found in the app store) called SmartRecycle. This app uses machine learning to tell recycling, from trash, from compost! With this I git 1st in my county science fair, and second in states!
